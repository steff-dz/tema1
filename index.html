THE FOUR PILLARS OF
THE GREEN PARTY

Falling wages and rising bills are hitting most of us, and the most vulnerable are hit the hardest. We demand a living wage and a real safety net. We work for universal healthcare, free higher education, human rights for all immigrants, full reproductive rights for women, human rights for all immigrants and for LGBTQs, and racial justice: Black Lives Matter!

We demand public financing of elections, open debates, and more representative voting systems. Greens will lead the opposition to the Trump-Pence agenda, and we will not comrpromise, capitulate or go along with bipartisan consensus. And we accept no corporate contributions!

Our country's long wars and worldwide military presence are immoral and unsustainable. Our military budget must be cut dramatically.

The human cost of climate change is too high. We need to get off fossil fuels and on to renewable energy.
